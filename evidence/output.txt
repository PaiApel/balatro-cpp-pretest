========================================
       Welcome to the Game!
========================================
  A card-based roguelike game

  HOW TO PLAY:
  - Beat the score target each round
  - Play poker hands to score points
  - Buy modifiers to boost your score
  - Survive as long as you can!

  CONTROLS:
  - [numbers]   = play selected cards
  - [numbers] D = discard selected cards
  - RS = sort by rank   | SS = sort by suit
  - H  = hand reference | Q  = quit

  SCORING:
  - Score = (Base Chips + Card Chips) x Mult
  - Modifiers applied after base score
  - Order of modifiers matters!
